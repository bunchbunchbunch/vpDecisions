## Description (4000 characters max)

VP Academy is the training app for video poker players who want to play with confidence. Whether you're learning the basics or sharpening your edge, VP Academy teaches you the math behind every decision.

TRAINING LESSONS
Work through 16 structured lessons covering Jacks or Better strategy from the ground up. Each lesson explains the key concept, highlights common mistakes, and ends with a quiz on real hands. Topics range from dealt hands that pay to straight draws, flush penalties, and advanced high card decisions.

PLAY MODE
Practice full video poker hands on a realistic Jacks or Better 9/6 machine. After every hand, VP Academy shows whether your hold was optimal and ranks every possible hold by expected value. See exactly how much each mistake costs — and why the correct play is correct.

HAND ANALYZER
Enter any 5-card hand and instantly see the mathematically optimal hold with a full EV breakdown. Compare every possible hold combination ranked by expected value. Use it to settle debates, study tricky hands, or verify your instincts.

QUIZ MODE
Test your strategy knowledge with targeted quizzes. Get immediate feedback on each hand and track your accuracy over time.

STUDY HALL
Access all lessons, drills, and review queues in one place. Track your progress across the full curriculum and see where you need more practice.

KEY FEATURES
- 16 structured lessons covering complete Jacks or Better strategy
- Real-time feedback on every hand you play
- Full expected value breakdown for all 32 hold combinations
- Hand analyzer for studying any specific hand
- Works offline — no internet required for core features
- Dark theme designed for comfortable play

BUILT FOR SERIOUS PLAYERS
VP Academy is focused on teaching, not gambling. There are no coins to buy, no ads to watch, and no fake casino mechanics. Every feature is designed to help you understand why the optimal play is optimal — not just what it is.

Whether you play recreationally or visit casinos regularly, understanding optimal strategy is the single biggest edge you can give yourself. VP Academy makes that knowledge accessible and easy to retain.
