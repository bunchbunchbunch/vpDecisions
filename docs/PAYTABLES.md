## Notes

1. **RF** = Royal Flush, **SF** = Straight Flush, **4K** = Four of a Kind
2. **FH** = Full House, **FL** = Flush, **ST** = Straight
3. **3K** = Three of a Kind, **2P** = Two Pair, **JoB** = Jacks or Better
4. **NRF** = Natural Royal Flush, **WRF** = Wild Royal Flush
5. **4D** = Four Deuces, **5K** = Five of a Kind
6. **+k** = with kicker bonus (specific fifth card)
7. All payouts shown are for 1 coin bet (multiply by 5 for max bet, except Royal which is typically 4000)
8. Return percentages assume optimal strategy play
